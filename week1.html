Human-Powered-Machine

Design and build a human-powered machine that performs a practical function without using electricity.

This could include lifting a small weight, pumping water, grinding grain,or transporting an object.

Your machine should demonstrate principles of mechanical advantage, energy efficiency, and user-centered design.

This challenge blends physics with creativity and problem-solving. The goal is not just to make something that
works but something that works well, is intuitive to use, and reflects thoughtful design.

Requirements

  • The machine must be entirely powered by human effort (e.g., pushing, pulling, turning).
  • Must perform a useful task (e.g., lift, move, rotate, grind, compress).
  • Use simple machines (e.g., levers, pulleys, gears, screws, wheels, inclined planes).
  • Prioritize safety, durability, and ease of use.
  • Maximize mechanical advantage and minimize wasted effort.

Problem Solving Questions

Your mother pays you 12 GHS per hour to babysit your sister, Tachema, on weekends. You offer your brother
10% of your weekend salary to cover Saturday night from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m.Requirements

  • How much do you earn for the entire weekend?
  • How much do you pay your brother and his wife?
  • How much do you keep?++++

Answers

How much do you earn for the entire weekend?

Assumption

*She babysitted for 48 hours

In 1 hour she earns 12 GHS, what about 48 hours.

48 * 12 = 576 GHS

How much do you pay your brother and his wife?

10/100 * 576 = 57.6 GHS

How much do you keep?

576 - 57.6 = 518.4 GHS

Estimate the number of videos
watched on YouTube per day.

  • Recent estimates, YouTube has over 2 billion logged-in monthly users.
  • Assume about 30% of these users are active daily. This gives us
    approximately 600 million daily users.
  • If each user watches an average of 5 videos per day,
    600 million x 5 videos = 3 billion videos per day.
  • Estimated Videos Watched per Day: 3 billion videos.

How much money does the
Play Store make in a year?

  • Assuming the Play Store has a share of 30% of total app-generated income,
    including both iOS and Android apps, 170 billion dollars.
  • 170 billion * 0.30 = 51 billion.
  • The Play Store also earns from in-app purchases and subscriptions, which could
    add another significant amount.
  • Estimated Annual Revenue for Play Store: Approximately $50-60 billion.




What is the market size for driverless cars by 2028?

The initial market was valued at around $54 billion in 2022.

If the growth Rate of the market is expected to grow at a CAGR of about 20%

Time period=6

We can use future value formula(FV = PV x (1 + r)^n)

PV = 54 billion dollars

r = 0.20 (20% growth)

n = 6 years

FV = 54 * (1 + 0.20)^6

54 * 2.98598=161.4 billion estimated market size
